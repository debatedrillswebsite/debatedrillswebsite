What is included

This is like math tutoring, where we go over specific problems the student is having with debate, be it technical (speaking faster, speaking more efficiently) or substantive (trouble executing a certain debate, such as util debates, framework debates, theory debates or critical debates).

Our teaching method is that we will read an argument, mimic a real debate round and have the student respond to that argument, and then go over their responses with them and help them understand how to improve. Homework will be giving the improved speech next time we have a session. We usually get through 2-3 skills in one session, and there are hundreds of skills that are necessary to compete at a high level, so the longer and more consistently the student works, the better they will get.

This method is very successful because it implements the philosophy of “do it yourself.” We focus on having my students give actual speeches and respond to arguments, and then see immediate improvement, which a) increases their comfort with the skill in question and b) improves their confidence.
